Local code repo
   ↓
Indexer reads files
   ↓
Files split into line-based chunks
   ↓
Local embedding model converts chunks into vectors
   ↓
Qdrant stores vectors + metadata
   ↓
Retriever searches relevant chunks
   ↓
Ollama generates answers from retrieved context
